# Future Spinner, Submission Blurb

**Game:** Future Spinner
**Studio:** We Roll Spinners

## Short description (for the Stake Engine review form)

Future Spinner is a cyberpunk ways-to-win slot set in a rain-soaked neon megacity of high-octane automotive chaos. Spin a 5 by 4 grid with 1,024 ways to win: match symbols on adjacent reels from the left, with Wilds substituting for every paying symbol. Land 3, 4, or 5 Scatters anywhere for an instant 1x, 3x, or 10x multiplier on your total stake, stacked on top of any ways win in the same spin. The game is fully stateless with no bonus rounds or held state, every result is decided by the Remote Game Server, and the top win is capped at 5,000x.

- Grid: 5 reels by 4 rows, 1,024 ways
- Theoretical RTP: 96.35%
- Maximum win: 5,000x
- Volatility: medium to high
- Special feature: instant Scatter multiplier (1x / 3x / 10x), stateless
- Min bet: 0.10, Max bet: 100.00

## One-liner

Cyberpunk 1,024-ways spinners with an instant Scatter multiplier and a 5,000x ceiling, 96.35% RTP.
